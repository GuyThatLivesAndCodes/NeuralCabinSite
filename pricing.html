Common questions

If we missed yours, ask on GitHub Discussions.

Is the desktop version still free?
Yes — and it always will be. The open-source workbench you download today is GPL-3.0 with no usage limits. Cloud is a separate, optional product for people who would rather not run their own machine.
What does "preview pricing" mean?
NeuralCabin Cloud is not generally available yet. Anyone who joins during preview locks in the rate above for as long as they stay subscribed. When we launch publicly, new sign-ups may pay more.
What GPUs do you use?
Workloads run on consumer-class GPUs from our VPS partner — currently L4 / A10 class, with bursts to higher-tier cards for Studio. We do not run on H100s today; the engine is being tuned for cost-efficient training rather than raw FLOPs.
Can I move my work between cloud and desktop?
Yes. Every network, corpus, and vocabulary can be exported as JSON checkpoints or GGUF files and pulled into the desktop app. You are never locked in.
What happens when I hit a limit?
Training pauses and you get a notification. Nothing auto-upgrades or auto-charges. You decide: upgrade, wait until next cycle, or export the checkpoint and continue locally on the free desktop app.
Refunds?
Within 14 days of your first charge, full refund, no questions. After that, prorated for unused time on yearly plans.
